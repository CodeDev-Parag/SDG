Step-by-Step Guide

How to Apply for Scholarships

The NSP portal is your gateway to most central government scholarships. Follow these steps.

1

Register on NSP

Visit scholarships.gov.in and create a new student account with your Aadhaar number and mobile.

2

Fill Your Profile

Enter educational, family income, and community details accurately. Upload required documents.

3

Select Schemes

The portal shows all schemes you qualify for. Apply to all eligible ones — no limit on applications.

4

Track & Renew

Track status on NSP dashboard. Most scholarships require annual renewal — mark your calendar.

Ready to Apply?

All major central government scholarships can be applied to through a single portal. Don't wait — deadlines close every year between August and November.
