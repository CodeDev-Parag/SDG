Education Platforms

Discover 15+ free Indian and global platforms offering thousands of courses — from school to postgraduate, in multiple languages.

Did you know? All Indian government platforms are completely free. Most are accessible on mobile and available in multiple regional languages. No credit card or payment required — ever.
🎓
