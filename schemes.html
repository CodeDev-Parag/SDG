Education Schemes

India's most impactful government schemes supporting school education, higher learning, girl child education, and skill development.

⚠️ Disclaimer: Information sourced from official government portals. Always verify details on the original site before applying. Report outdated info.
🏛️

National Scholarship Portal (NSP) — Your First Stop

Over 100+ central and state scholarships in one place. Annual disbursement of ₹1,000 to ₹2 lakh per student depending on the scheme. Over 1.5 crore students benefit every year. If you qualify for any scheme below, apply through NSP first.

Visit scholarships.gov.in →
🏫

School Education

5 Schemes
Fundamental Right

Right to Education (RTE) Act, 2009

Guarantees free and compulsory education to every child aged 6–14 years in India. Schools cannot charge fees. Private schools must reserve 25% seats for economically weaker sections.

Coverage All children aged 6–14 years, nationally
Infrastructure & Quality

Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan

Umbrella scheme for school education providing universal access, teacher training, digital classrooms, and school infrastructure support from pre-school to Class 12.

Focus Area School infrastructure, teacher training, digital tools
Nutrition & Retention

PM Poshan (Mid-Day Meal Scheme)

Free nutritious cooked meals to school children in government and aided schools. Reduces dropout rates significantly. One of the world's largest school feeding programs.

Beneficiaries 12+ crore school children annually
Girls' Education

Kasturba Gandhi Balika Vidyalaya (KGBV)

Residential schools for girls from SC, ST, OBC, and minority backgrounds. Classes 6–12 with full boarding, meals, uniforms, and books — completely free.

Target Group Girls from disadvantaged communities, Classes 6–12
Girl Child

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

National campaign addressing gender bias and promoting education and welfare of the girl child. Works through awareness drives, incentives, and community mobilization.

Goal Improve female literacy and reduce girl-child dropout

Higher Education

5 Schemes
Education Loans

PM Vidyalakshmi Scheme

Single-window portal connecting students to 38+ banks for education loans. Simplified application process with transparent tracking. Covers tuition, hostel, books, and living expenses.

Loan Amount Up to ₹7.5 lakh (collateral-free) and above with collateral
Girls in Tech

AICTE Pragati Scholarship

Annual financial aid for girl students pursuing technical education (engineering, architecture, pharmacy) in AICTE-approved institutions. One per family.

Amount ₹50,000 per year + ₹2,000 contingency
Merit & Need

National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS)

For meritorious students from economically weaker sections. Selected through a state-level examination. Covers Classes 9 to 12 to prevent dropout at the secondary stage.

Amount ₹12,000 per year (₹1,000/month)
SC · ST · OBC

Post-Matric Scholarship (SC/ST/OBC)

Financial aid for students from marginalized communities pursuing education after Class 10. Covers tuition fees, maintenance allowance, and study tour charges. State-administered.

Eligibility SC/ST/OBC students post-Class 10; income limits apply
Academic Merit

Central Sector Scheme of Scholarships (CSSS)

For students who scored in the top 20 percentile in Class 12 board exams. Applicable for undergraduate and postgraduate studies in recognized colleges.

Amount ₹10,000–₹20,000 per year
Minority Communities

Merit-cum-Means Scholarship (Minorities)

For students from minority communities (Muslim, Christian, Sikh, Buddhist, Jain, Parsi) pursuing professional and technical courses. Ministry of Minority Affairs manages disbursement.

Amount Up to ₹30,000 per year course fee + ₹10,000 maintenance

Skill Development & Employment

4 Schemes
NSDC · Free Certification

PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Free skill training and certification in 30+ sectors including IT, healthcare, construction, retail, and manufacturing. Implemented by NSDC through training partners across India.

Benefit Free training + certificate + job placement support
AI · Cybersecurity · Data

Digital India Program

Free courses in AI, cybersecurity, data science, and digital tools. Aims to create 1 million digitally skilled youth. Accessible through multiple online and offline centers.

Courses AI, Cloud, Cyber Security, Coding, Data Science
Entrepreneurship

Startup India Learning Program

Free entrepreneurship, business planning, and startup management courses. Learn from successful founders and government mentors. Certificates recognized by DPIIT.

Focus Business skills, product thinking, startup operations
Electronics · IT

NIELIT Courses

National Institute of Electronics and IT offers free and heavily subsidized courses in O, A, B, C level computer applications, cybersecurity, data analytics, and IoT.

Course Fee Free to ₹500 (heavily subsidized)

A centralized awareness platform dedicated to bridging the education knowledge gap in India.
